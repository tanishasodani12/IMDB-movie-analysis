# IMDB-movie-analysis

This project analyzes IMDb movie data to understand which factors influence audience ratings and movie performance. The focus was on how **genre**, **director**, **language**, **duration**, and **budget** impact IMDb ratings and profitability.

## üìä Key Results & Insights
- **Film-Noir and Biography** genres had the **highest average ratings**.
- **Movie duration has almost no relationship** with ratings (very weak correlation).
- **Regional languages** such as **Telugu and Polish** had higher average ratings compared to widely released English-language films.
- **Top-performing directors consistently achieve higher audience ratings**, indicating the role of creative direction.
- **Higher budgets did not guarantee higher profit** ‚Äî cost efficiency and content quality matter more.

---

## ‚≠ê Final Takeaways
- **Genre + Director quality** influences rating far more than runtime or budget.
- **Strong direction and storytelling** outperform expensive production.
- Smaller, regional films can outperform mainstream films when content is strong.
